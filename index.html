About

I work at the intersection of business operations and product. On the operating side, that means sales strategy, process design, and the project management that turns a plan into a shipped outcome. On the product side, it means picking problems I find genuinely interesting and building until they work.

I head sales, business, and operations at Spazorlabs, and act as project manager on its larger initiatives. I co-founded 6Pistons Media, which started as a passion project helping car dealerships market themselves and now runs as a revenue business. I also consult on DevOps for Strive Consultancy.

Outside client work, I build things for fun: a blockchain-based carbon credit system, and a multi-radio exposure mapper for wireless security, both with patents in process.
